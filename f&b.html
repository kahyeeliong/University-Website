EXPLORING FOODS AND DRINKS AT UNIVERSITY OF SHEFFIELD

Discover the best places to eat, relax, and enjoy your time on our beautiful campus.

Wave Kitchen

Wave Kitchen, found on the ground floor of the Wave building, offers wide range of freshly made hot & cold food & drink.

Opening Hours: Monday to Friday 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM

View on Google Maps

Heartspace Cafe

Heartspace Cafe is located on the ground floor of the Engineering atrium, offering a huge variety of sweet treats, quality coffee and delivious hot and cold food.

Opening Hours: Monday to Friday 8:00 AM - 2:30 PM

View on Google Maps

Solly Street Kitchen

Try our sizzling chicken in black bean sauce, fresh from the wok with noodles and flavourful vegetables, as well as many other international foods offering everyone a piece of home.

Opening Hours: Monday to Sunday 8:00 AM - 2:00 PM

View on Google Maps

Arts Tower Cafe

Nestled in the basement of the Arts Tower building. Home of the famous paternoster lift, the Arts Tower also boasts this excellent cafe, featuring a range of tasty treasures fresh out of the oven and served all day. We serve coffee and cake alongside comfort food like quiches or jacket potatoes with a choice of toppings to suit everyone.

Opening Hours: Monday to Friday 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM

View on Google Maps

Grill & Go

Head to the Students' Union for a taste of Mexico with our famous wraps, salads, and burritos.

Opening Hours: Monday to Friday 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Diamond Kitchen

Grab a bite to eat in the Diamond Building – the University’s largest investment in teaching. The Diamond Kitchen offers a wonderful array of international flavours with a fantastic grab-and-go offer, complemented by a sleek and stylish design.

Opening Hours: Monday to Friday 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM

View on Google Maps

Jessop Cafe

Jessop Café features a wide range of hot drinks and food prepared in house, such as poached eggs and avocado for a delicious healthy breakfast, or cakes and pastries for a lighter afternoon snack. On those rare occasions when the British weather permits, we have the perfect sun trap just outside!

Opening Hours: Monday to Friday 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

University Arms

Enjoy traditional pub food and a range of delicious local beers at the University Arms.

Opening Hours: Monday to Friday 12:00 PM - 11:00 PM & Saturday 4:00 PM - 11:00 PM

IC Café

The IC Café serves hot meals and snacks throughout the day and night. We offer curries with rice or chips to eat in or take away, a range of burgers, plus a hot dish of the day special. The IC Café also sells sandwiches, wraps, snacks, hot drinks and soft drinks so come in and grab something to eat anytime.

Opening Hours: Monday to Sunday 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM

View on Google Maps

Inox

Inox is a flexible event space located on the fifth floor of the University of Sheffield Students' Union Building. We provide all day dining, venue hire and a place for meetings and social working. Book a table today or drop in and see Inox for yourself.

Opening Hours: Monday to Friday 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

View on Google Maps

Edge Bar

Come in for a bite to eat from our modern pub grub inspired menu featuring pizzas, burgers, chicken and loaded fries, or grab a few drinks with friends and chill out with live sport, pool tables, darts, music and more.

Opening Hours: Monday to Sunday 4:00 PM - 12:00 AM

The Village Store

The Village Store has all the essentials you need, such as fresh fruit and vegetables, groceries, frozen food, magazines and toiletries. Stocking all your favourite brands and locally sourced produce, including Our Cow Molly milk and ice cream.

Opening Hours: Monday to Saturday 8:00 AM - 11:00 PM & Sunday 11:00 AM - 8:00 PM

View on Google Maps

Cafe 1828

A modern and much loved cafe located in the heart of the Medical School. Cafe 1828 features a great range of coffees, snack, salads and hot food.

Opening Hours: Monday to Friday 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM

View on Google Maps

AMRC Cafe

Found inside the Advanced Manufacturing Research Centre Training Centre. With hot food available throughout the day, along with snacks, sandwiches and hot drinks, the AMRC Café is perfect spot to grab a hearty breakfast or filling lunch to get you through the working day.

Available on Fridays, the AMRC Café offers a delicious cooked breakfast. You can get 2 bacon rashers, 2 sausages, 2 hash browns, 2 eggs, beans, tomatoes and toast for just £3.95. This full English is not to be missed!

Opening Hours: Monday to Friday 8:00 AM - 1:00 PM
