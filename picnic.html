Top Picnic Spots Near University of Sheffield

Looking for a place to relax and enjoy a meal outdoors? Here are some of the best picnic locations near campus.

Weston Park

Weston Park is a tranquil green space perfect for picnics and a favorite among students. The park features a picturesque lake, lush lawns, and plenty of spots to relax.

Fun Fact: Weston Park is home to the Weston Park Museum, which offers free entry to visitors. It’s also conveniently located just a 10-minute walk from the University of Sheffield tram stop!

Endcliffe Park

Endcliffe Park is a popular picnic spot, known for its picturesque streams and proximity to Ecclesall Road's cafes and shops. Perfect for a relaxed afternoon.

Fun Fact: The nearby Ecclesall Road has loads of independent restaurants and cafes to choose from, including the vegan Pom Kitchen, which is now selling picnic boxes so that you can share a boujee picnic with your mates.

The Botanical Gardens

The Botanical Gardens are just down the road from Endcliffe Park but are also a prime picnic spot. The cafe does great coffee, which you can enjoy whilst sitting amongst plants from all over the world.

Fun Fact: The best spot in the springtime is just in front of the beautiful crocuses.

Crookes Valley Park

Adjacent to Weston Park, Crookes Valley Park features a large lake and plenty of open green space.
