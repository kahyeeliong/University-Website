About This Website

Hello! My name is Kah Yee, and I'm a student at the University of Sheffield. This website was created to provide a comprehensive guide for students, visitors, and staff to navigate our beautiful campus.

From the best food and drink vendors to picturesque picnic locations, this platform aims to highlight must-visit spots and hidden gems around the university. Whether you are new to Sheffield or a long-time member of the community, I hope you find this guide useful and engaging.
